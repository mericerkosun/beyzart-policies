Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do credits work?

Your subscription includes a weekly allowance of credits. Each image you generate (either from a photo or text) uses one credit. Your credits automatically reset every week, based on your subscription renewal date.

How can I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription, including changing plans or canceling, directly through your Apple Account settings. Go to your device's Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.

How do I get the best results?

For best results, use clear, well-lit photos where the subject's face is clearly visible. For text-to-image, be descriptive! For example, instead of "a cat," try "a fluffy white cat with blue eyes sitting on a windowsill."

Is my data safe?

Yes. We take your privacy seriously. We do not store your uploaded photos on our servers after the AI transformation is complete. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
