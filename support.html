Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do subscriptions and credits work?

Our app requires a subscription for image generation. We offer Weekly and Annual plans. Each plan provides a set number of credits (e.g., 30 for Weekly, 300 for Annual) that are renewed at the start of each billing cycle. Unused credits do not roll over.

How can I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription, including changing plans or canceling, directly through your Apple Account settings. On your device, go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.

What happens if I run out of credits?

If you are an active subscriber and run out of credits, you will need to wait until your next renewal date for your credits to be replenished. The renewal date is shown in your subscription settings.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We take your privacy seriously. We do not store your uploaded photos on our servers after the AI transformation is complete. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
