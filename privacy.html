Privacy Policy for BeyzArt

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

Welcome to BeyzArt ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that is essential to provide and improve our service.

A. Information You Provide to Us:

  • Images and Photos: When you upload an image to be transformed, we process that image to provide our AI generation service.
  • Text Prompts: When you use our text-to-image or other text-based features, we process the text you provide.

B. Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Transaction Information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. We receive transaction data such as your subscription tier (e.g., "lite_weekly," "pro_weekly") and its status. We do not collect or store your payment card details.
  • Usage and Device Information: We automatically collect an anonymous RevenueCat Customer ID to manage your account, credits, and subscription status. We also collect non-personally identifiable device info (model, OS, app version) to help us troubleshoot and improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your uploaded images and text prompts using AI models.
  • To manage your subscription, assign and track your credits, and enable premium features.
  • To understand usage patterns and improve the App.
  • To respond to support requests or feedback.

3. How We Share Your Information

We only share the information necessary for third-party services to operate:

  • AI Service Providers: Google (Gemini/Imagen) and OpenAI (GPT) to generate artwork. Data usage is governed by their privacy policies.
  • Supabase: For backend storage of anonymous user ID, credits, and subscription status.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription validation and access control.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or legal process.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Images you create are stored locally on your device and can be deleted from the “My Creations” section.
  • Data sent to AI services is subject to their retention policies.
  • Anonymous account data is stored in Supabase as long as you use the App.

5. Your Rights

You may delete generated images at any time. For other data concerns, contact us.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted within the App.
