Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how BeyzArt ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our iOS application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our Service:

  • User-Provided Content: We process the photos you upload and the text prompts you enter to generate artistic images. We do not store your photos or text prompts on our servers after the generation process is complete.
  • Purchase Information: To manage your subscription, our third-party partner, RevenueCat, processes your purchase history. We do not have access to your payment information, such as credit card numbers.
  • Identifiers:
    • User ID: We assign a unique, anonymous identifier to your account, provided by RevenueCat, to manage your subscription status and credit balance.
    • Device ID: We collect your device model and operating system version to help with customer support and to analyze and improve app performance.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the App, such as which features you use. We also track the total number of images you generate ("Total Creations") to ensure the stability of our service and for internal analytics.
  • Diagnostics: We collect crash logs and performance data to identify and fix bugs and to improve the overall performance of the App.
  • Customer Support: If you contact us for support, we collect the information you provide in your communication, including your email address and the content of your message, to assist you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate the core functionality of the App, such as generating images, managing your subscription, and tracking your credits.
  • To Improve the App: To understand how our users interact with the App, to identify popular features, to diagnose and fix technical issues, and to plan for new features.
  • To Provide Customer Support: To respond to your questions, feedback, and requests.

3. Third-Party Partners

We use the following third-party services to operate our App. These services have their own privacy policies and are responsible for the data they process.

  • RevenueCat: Manages subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • Supabase: Provides our backend infrastructure, including database storage and edge functions.
  • Google & OpenAI: Provide the AI models used for image generation. Your uploaded photos and text prompts are sent to these services for processing.

4. Your Privacy Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access and request the deletion of the personal data we hold about you. You can submit a data deletion request by contacting us through the "Email Developer" option in the App's settings or by emailing us directly at meric.erkosun@icloud.com. Please provide your User ID (found in the App's settings) to help us locate your data.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is ever completely secure or error-free.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information from our files as soon as possible.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at meric.erkosun@icloud.com.
