Privacy Policy for BeyzArt

Last Updated: July 24, 2025

BeyzArt ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the BeyzArt mobile application (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We design our Service to be as private as possible.

Information We Do Collect:

  • Anonymous User ID: We use services from RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat assigns a randomized, anonymous identifier to you. This ID is not linked to your personal identity and is used solely to manage your subscription status and credit balance.
  • Device Information: We collect basic, non-identifying device information (e.g., device model, OS version) for analytics and to improve our Service. This is collected via our backend service (Supabase) and is tied to your anonymous ID.
  • Transaction Information: Purchase and subscription events are processed by RevenueCat and the Apple App Store. We receive anonymous transaction data (like subscription tier and renewal dates) to provide you with the services you've purchased. We do not see or store your payment information.

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not ask for, collect, or store any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your Photos and Generated Images: We cannot see, access, or store your photos or the images you generate. When you upload a photo or provide a text prompt, it is sent directly to our third-party AI service providers (OpenAI and Google) for processing. The resulting image is sent back directly to your device. Your gallery of created images is stored only on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited, anonymous information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Service.
  • Manage your subscription and credit balance.
  • Improve the Service and troubleshoot issues.
  • Monitor for compliance with our Terms of Service.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to provide our core functionality. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Apple App Store: Manages all payments and subscriptions.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status and anonymous user IDs.
  • Supabase: Our backend provider for managing anonymous user data (like credit balance) and proxying requests to AI services.
  • OpenAI & Google (Gemini/Imagen): The AI services that process your images and text prompts. They do not retain your data long-term and use it only to provide the service. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

4. Data Security

We use Supabase for our backend, which employs industry-standard security measures to protect the anonymous data we store. Since we do not collect personal information or your images, the risk of a personal data breach is significantly minimized.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. This is to comply with the terms of our third-party AI providers and with regulations like the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.
